Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this videography website is to showcase a videographer's talent and artistry. As a budding videographer I aim to help couples envision how the client can capture their special day. The site will serve as a portfolio to highlight the client's work, build credibility, and attract potential clients by sharing their unique storytelling style.

Audience

The audience for this site is couples looking for a videographer to capture their wedding day. It will largely be people local to the client's area but could expand to a larger geographical area as they gain experience. Weddings for people in all walks and stages of life should be drawn to see how a wedding video can help enhance their big day.

Dynamic elements

Javascript will be used to create an inquires form that users can use to reach an and book the videographer. I will aslo use an object to pull the information for each of the different packages. If I have the time I will also create a interactive booking calendar to show when the client is available.
